## Conclusion

All 13 vulnerabilities are theoretically applicable to Joomla 4.4.13 but **none are practically exploitable** without valid credentials. The com_finder reflected XSS (Vuln 5) is the only one approachable without authentication but is neutered by the default tokenizer.
